Why this show

Show Mission

Every show at Encore has a "why." It's the thing that connects the script to the lives of the young artists performing it. The mission below is written by the director and signed off by the Encore artistic team — it's the north star for every design choice, casting decision, and rehearsal note.

The Lion King is a show about what happens when we forget who we are — and what it takes to come home to ourselves. We're staging it for our young artists at a moment when many of them are wrestling with that exact question. This production is about belonging, responsibility, and the quiet courage it takes to step into a role that scares you.

The life skill we're teaching

This production's S.T.A.G.E. focus is resilience under pressure. Every rehearsal, every note, every tech glitch is a chance to practice the skill of staying grounded when things don't go as planned. We want every cast and crew member to leave this show able to say: "I held it together when it mattered."

What success looks like

A sold-out closing weekend. A cast that's still close six months from now. Three young artists who took on a role bigger than they thought they could carry — and did. A creative team that finished the run with more energy than they started with.

Show Notes

Things specific to this production that the whole team should be aware of. Update as you go — this lives in the Shows sheet and reflects in real time.

Sensory performance prep: Lighting cues that flash or strobe (Act II opening, "Be Prepared," final battle) must be flagged for the Oct 17 matinee and softened per the sensory-friendly guidelines. Coordinate with Riley by Oct 6.

ASL performance: Interpreter joining for the Oct 23 show. Stage-right placement, please leave the SR proscenium clear for sightlines. Pre-show meet & greet with the interpreter at 6:30 pm.

Costume note: Tomás is sourcing the Mufasa mantle from a partner organization in Salt Lake. Final fitting is Sep 28.

Parent meeting: Saturday Sep 5 at 10 am. Sarah, Marcus, and Devon attending. Parent info packet goes out by Aug 28.
